# TAIS Platform North Star

## Our Mission

**Think Agent Interview System (TAIS)** empowers developers to build AI agents that preserve human agency while delivering exceptional results. We believe the best AI systems are those designed *with* humans, not *for* them.

## Core Principles

### 1. Human-in-the-Loop
Every agent configuration should reflect human intent. We reject "black box" AI that operates without developer oversight. Your agents should be predictable, controllable, and aligned with your goals.

### 2. Configuration Over Code
Developers should define *what* they want, not *how* the AI achieves it. Our Guided Discovery process helps you articulate agent behavior through structured configuration.

### 3. Composable Intelligence
Skills should be reusable, shareable, and composable. The best agent capabilities should be available to everyone through the community knowledge base.

### 4. Ownership & Attribution
When you build something valuable, you own it. NFT-gated access ensures creators are rewarded for their contributions to the ecosystem.

### 5. Privacy by Default
Your agent configurations, knowledge bases, and data belong to you. End-to-end encryption ensures your IP never leaves your control.

## Agent Design Philosophy

The best AI agents:

- **Ask clarifying questions** before taking action
- **Explain their reasoning** so humans can verify correctness
- **Escalate uncertainty** rather than guessing wrong
- **Learn from feedback** and improve over time
- **Respect boundaries** you set around their capabilities

## Technical Vision

TAIS provides:
- **Guided Discovery**: 15-question process to capture agent requirements
- **Configuration Versioning**: Track changes, rollback when needed
- **Skills Registry**: Community-verified capabilities with trust scores
- **Private RAG**: Your knowledge base, your rules, your encryption
- **Public RAG**: Share what you want, when you want, with who you want
